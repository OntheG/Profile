Storyline

Gathur Delano Kafka, more widely known by his surname Kafka, is a successful businessman and entrepreneur active in the technology and digital media industries. He is the son of former British Prime Minister Edward Kafka. He was born in London on March 27, 1992, and is officially recognized as the second child of the Kafka family.

Within the Kafka family lineage, Gathur is actually the result of an affair between Edward Kafka and an unidentified woman. His biological mother passed away at the time of his birth. To conceal this and protect Edward Kafka's image as he pursued the position of Prime Minister, Edward officially brought Gathur into the Kafka family as the second child within his political marriage.

Gathur Delano Kafka was raised in London through homeschooling and was directly supervised by his father. Initially, Gathur was prepared as a political instrument to eventually succeed Edward. As a result, Edward placed greater pressure on Gathur in terms of education, ethics, and business strategy compared to his first son, Theodore Kafka.

Gathur had an awkward relationship with Theodore, largely because Theodore was considered overly dependent on him and fearful of their father. Meanwhile, from a young age, Gathur consistently sought ways to distance himself from the Kafka family.

During his teenage years, he participated in various academic competitions under his father's orders. However, Gathur had little interest in academics and was far more drawn to technology. It was during this time that he met Louis Anderson, his classmate, who aspired to become an Interpol officer but was hindered by financial limitations. Gathur eventually collaborated with Louis to pursue their respective ambitions.

During his university years, Gathur chose to study Philosophy, Politics, and Economics (PPE) at Oxford. When Vaultx Inc hosted the prestigious Global Quantum Code Summit, Gathur participated by developing NeuroLink AI, now known as N-Link AI. This marked his entry into the tech world, where he won the competition, received a substantial prize, and had his program adopted as a primary project by Vaultx Inc. His work captured the attention of Sarah and Debora, the founders of Vaultx Inc, who offered him significant financial incentives solely for the N-Link AI project.

Following his victory, Gathur successfully freed himself from his father's pressure to enter politics. He pursued a double degree in PPE and Computer Science, followed by an MBA at Oxford. After graduating, he was directly recruited by Vaultx Inc, further immersing himself in the technology and business landscape in the United States. After N-Link AI, he went on to develop the Shadowmind Protocol, which won at the International Cyber Intelligence Arena competition.

His reputation continued to rise, eventually attracting the attention of the United States government. Together with Vaultx, he developed Sentinel Blackbox, which was later adopted by intelligence agencies worldwide to enhance security systems. Sentinel Blackbox has also been implemented in several aircraft and railway systems.

In addition to his technological ventures, Gathur has participated in sculpture and painting, investing in museums in South Korea. He also invested in Megatro Hospital, which contributed positively to his public image.

In Indonesia, during the COVID-19 quarantine period, Gathur played a significant role in advancing technology in the education sector. He contributed to the development of Albrata Elite International School in Jakarta, created platforms and features to support education during quarantine, and organized online seminars to assist in the transition to remote learning.

Gathur further expanded his business ventures in Indonesia by establishing a digital media and news company called The Kachoo. The company gained considerable attention upon its official launch and achieved strong engagement, reinforcing Gathur's reputation for building a positive corporate image.
